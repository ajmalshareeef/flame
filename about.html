Our Founding

Flame Learnings was founded with the mission of making quality education accessible to every student, regardless of location. We specialize in online tuition for grades 9 through 12 and Montessori teacher training programs.

Growth & Beyond

Since our inception, Flame Learnings has helped hundreds of students excel academically and teachers enhance their skills. Our commitment to growth ensures that we are always evolving with the latest in educational methods and technology.
